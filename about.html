About us

Niograph is an Application development, Technology services and IT-consulting company with expertise in cutting-edge technologies. We build solutions for our clients by leveraging latest technologies. We are coders, engineers, consultants and technology experts united by a common theme - Passion for Technology!

We love to work on complex problems!

It is how we've accomplished amazing things, and it is how we help our customers and partners achieve success!

We are now a strong team of 15+ members working from different geographical locations and working for the common theme - Passion for Technology!


How it started?

It all started back in May 2014 - when a small group of technology enthusiasts started working on a project which was mere a hobby project at that time. It took them fews months to develop the solution and was delivered to customer. The customer appreciated the work and the dedication put in. The solution was scalable, fast, handled thousands of users and required minimal support.

The efforts were taking shape! After achieving initial recognition and good feedback from customer, the group thought to proceed further with the idea. The members agreed to form a company so that they can start working in an organized way. They started working on the vision and mission and to define the the strategy. This was a time when the foundations of Niograph were being laid.


Niograph is BORN!

In 2015, we approached to a few angel investors who got interested in the idea of Niograph. They were ready to engage funds! It was a moment when we felt that we can really turn things. We formed the company with the name of Niograph Technology Consulting LLP with its registered office in Indore, Madhya Pradesh - India. Niograph became a registered company in Feb- 2016!


Niograph Values

Niograph is built with a philosophy of Technology being the major driver in changing the world. Alongwith passion, commitment, teamwork and knowledge - Niograph believes in solving the most complex problems. We believe in delivering quality solution -in a timely, professional and cost effective manner. It is this attitude that makes our customers happy and we get opportunities to serve them again and again!
